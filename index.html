A Unique Sport — TEQBALL

Teqball is a unique combination of table tennis and soccer. It was invented in Hungary in 2012.

Like in table tennis, two to four players can play teqball at a time. They play it on a table with two sides and a glass divider in the middle. The curved table keeps the ball bouncing. Like in soccer, players play with a soccer ball. They hit it with their body instead of a racket.

Teqball matches last for three sets. There are many ways to score. For example, players can get a point when an opponent touches the ball with their hand. They must reach twelve points first to win a set.

Teqball is fun and challenging. Try it sometime!
